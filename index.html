ABOUT US

Learning has always been the foundation of humanity and is one of the most essential parts of human life. Like education, technology is also a very important part of life.

As the 21st century progresses, we thought, why not combine education and technology to make quality education accessible to all from anywhere, at any time.

Our aim is to Provide E-learning services through our specialised platform "WiW Learn". We hope to make learning easy, fun and more seamless for students in post secondary and senior secondary levels and also for aspirants hoping to crack competitive examinations. Our method of teaching gives students a better and more immersive learning experience.

OUR PRODUCTS

We offer E-learning solutions and online classes for students through our platform, WiW learn.

WIW LEARN

In our Specialised learning app we provide classes for Kerala State Syllabus, ICSE and CBSE. Currently we have video classes for 12th grade students and will provide other classes in the near future. Subjects provided by us are Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. Our online visual learning classes are conceptualised and created by expert teachers and Subject matter experts.

We provide example tests on every topic, which are segregated into three difficulty levels (easy, medium, hard), our students can also access and solve sample questions from every chapter.

A student can also view their profile where analytics of their performances in past test papers will be shown and also the total time of classes attended. The student can also raise doubts on any topic. These doubts will be cleared by our Subject Matter Experts.
