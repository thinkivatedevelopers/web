ABOUT US

Learning has always been the foundation of humanity and is one of the most essential aspects of human life. Like education, technology is also an indispensable part of modern living.

At Thinkivate we thought why not combine learning and technology to make quality education accessible to anyone, anytime, anywhere? Therefore, we developed our specialized e-learning platform WIW Learn.

With WIW Learn we hope to make learning easy, fun and more seamless for students in post-secondary and senior secondary levels and also for aspirants hoping to crack competitive examinations.

OUR PRODUCTS

We offer E-learning solutions and online classes for students through our platform, WiW learn.

WIW LEARN

Through our specialized learning app we provide classes for Kerala State Syllabus, CBSE and ICSE. Currently we provide video classes for 12th grade students and shall include other grades in the near future. Subjects covered are Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. Our online visual learning classes are conceptualized and created by experienced teachers and subject matter experts. Students can raise doubts on any topic, which will be cleared by the experts.

We conduct example tests on each topic, which are divided into three levels (easy, medium and hard). Our students can also access and solve sample questions from every chapter.

Students can also view their profiles where analytics of their performances in past test papers and the total time of classes attended will be shown.
